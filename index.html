Oregon State

Oregon is a state located in the Pacific Northwest region of the United States. The area of Oregon is about 98,466 square miles and has a population of about 4.2 million people. Some of the state's most popular cities include Portland, Salem, Bend, Eugene, Astoria, Lincoln City, and Corvallis. In addition to the state’s cities, Oregon is notorious for its stunning scenery, including its coastline, mountains, rivers, and lakes. This state has a wide range of activities available such as hiking, biking, surfing, visiting historical landmarks, site seeing, and so much more.

  • [1] Fun facts about Oregon:
    • Official State Nut: Hazelnut
    • Official State Flower: Oregon Grape
    • Official State Bird: Western Meadowlark
    • Official State Tree: Douglas Fir
    • Highest Peak: Mount Hood (11,239')
