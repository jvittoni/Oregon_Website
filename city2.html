Eugene

A City of Oregon

Eugene is a city in Oregon. The city has a total area of 40.54 square miles. It is a city that offers both urban and suburban areas to its residents. While in Eugene, people enjoy going to the 5th Street Public Market, Eugene Saturday Market, Hult Center for the Performing Arts, Hendricks Park, Skinner Butte Park, and the Cascades Raptor Center.

  • The city’s population: 177,923 (2022) [1]

  • The year the city was incorporated: 1862 [2]

  • The region of the state in which the city is located: Southern End of Willamette Valley [3]

  • The classification of the city: urban, suburban, and/or rural: Urban and Suburban [4]

  • The average income level of the city compared to the rest of the state:
    • City's median household income: $61,481 (2022) [5]
    • City per capita income: $39,081 (2022) [6]
    • State average income: $64,018 (2021) [7]
