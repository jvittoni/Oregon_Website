Lincoln City

A City of Oregon

Lincoln City is a city in Oregon. The city has a total area of 6.085 square miles. It is a small town on the coast of Oregon with over seven miles of beach. Some of the popular things to do in Lincoln City include going to Roads End State Recreation Site, Connie Hansen Garden Conservancy, D River State Recreation Site, and the Lincoln City Beach.

  • The city’s population: 9,966 (2022) [1]

  • The year the city was incorporated: 1965 [2]

  • The region of the state in which the city is located: On the Oregon Coast in Lincoln County [3]

  • The classification of the city: urban, suburban, and/or rural: Rural [4]

  • The average income level of the city compared to the rest of the state:
    • City's median household income: $54,210 (2022) [5]
    • City's per capita income: $38,282 (2022) [6]
    • State average income: $64,018 (2021) [7]
